Natalie's Favorite Movies

Ranking My Top 5 Movies

1. The Adventures of Robin Hood (1938)

In my opinion the best Robin Hood movie of all time. This has been a favorite since I was 10 years old.

2. An Affair to Remember (1957)

Possibly the most purely romantic movie ever made.

3. Before Sunrise (1995)

Nothing makes me want to be young and/or traveling through Europe like this movie.

Captures that young adult feeling of new-found freedom, and honest, but pretentious philosophizing perfectly.

4. Persuassion (1995)

The best adaptation of my favorite Jane Austen novel.

5. Notorious (1946)

Spies, yearning, jealousy, romance, tension...all with absolutely perfect performances by Igrid Bergman and Cary Grant.
